Brand story

Every slice tells a story. Here's the one that started ours.

A pizza truck isn't where you'd expect to find a children's hospital waiting room. But that's where the idea for Slice Story actually came from: a parent, a vending machine sandwich, and hours that don't end. We thought about how something as small as a really good meal can be the one normal, good thing in a day that has nothing else normal or good in it. So we decided to build a business where that meal also says, quietly, someone's thinking of you.

That's why $1 from every pizza we sell, every single one, goes to local families facing childhood cancer. Not a slogan on a poster. A real dollar, every time, since the first day we opened the truck.

We still make the best pizza we know how to make: slow-fermented dough, real Italian ingredients, baked at 900 degrees the traditional way. Because the families counting on us deserve a business that's good at its job, not just good-hearted. Every slice tells a story. We hope yours, in some small way, becomes part of someone else's.
