Brand story

Great pizza should feel special, not out of reach.

Slice Story brings flame-baked Neapolitan-style pizza to weekday lunch breaks, markets, festivals, schools, hospitals, breweries, and private celebrations. The truck format makes premium pizza more accessible without losing the quality, warmth, or experience that makes it memorable.

Quality starts with the dough, sauce, and cheese. Caputo 00 flour creates a soft, airy crust with a lightly crisp edge. San Marzano DOP tomatoes bring a naturally sweet, balanced sauce. Fresh fior di latte mozzarella melts cleanly without making the pizza heavy or greasy.

High-heat flame baking gives each pizza its blistered character, gentle char, and aroma. It keeps the centre tender, brings the edge to life, and creates a texture standard fast-food pizza often cannot match.
